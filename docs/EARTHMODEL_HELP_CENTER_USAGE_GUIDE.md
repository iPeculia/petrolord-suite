# User Guide: EarthModel Pro Help Center

## How to Access
*   **Click:** The question mark icon (?) in the top right header.
*   **Keyboard:** Press `F1` anywhere in the application.
*   **Menu:** "Help & Support" in the User Profile menu.

## Searching
1.  Type your query in the search bar at the top of the Help panel.
2.  As you type, suggested articles will appear.
3.  Press `Enter` to see full results categorized by type (Articles, FAQs, Videos).

## Browsing Topics
*   Use the **Categories** grid on the home screen to explore topics like "Structural Modeling" or "Petrophysics".
*   Click a category to see a list of all related articles.

## Using Articles
*   **Navigation:** Use the Table of Contents at the top (if available) to jump to sections.
*   **Feedback:** Click "Yes" or "No" at the bottom to let us know if the article helped.
*   **Related:** Check the "Related Articles" section for further reading.

## Video Tutorials
*   Navigate to the **Video Library** section.
*   Videos open in a modal player.
*   Most videos include closed captions and transcripts.

## Getting Support
If you can't find an answer:
1.  Click the **"Contact Support"** button at the bottom of the Help panel.
2.  Fill out the form with your issue details.
3.  A support ticket will be created, and you will be notified via email.
