# Case Studies

## Case Study 1: Rapid Onboarding at GeoCorp
*   **Challenge**: New geoscientists took 3 weeks to become productive.
*   **Solution**: Implemented role-based Learning Paths in the Training System.
*   **Result**: Time-to-productivity reduced to 5 days (80% improvement).

## Case Study 2: Ticket Deflection at DrillCo
*   **Challenge**: Support team overwhelmed by "How-to" questions.
*   **Solution**: Deployed Contextual Help and AI Chatbot.
*   **Result**: 60% reduction in L1 support tickets; agents focused on complex issues.

## Case Study 3: Engagement Boost at EnergyX
*   **Challenge**: Advanced features were underutilized.
*   **Solution**: Launched "Feature Explorer" badges and leaderboards.
*   **Result**: 40% increase in daily active usage of advanced modeling tools.

## Case Study 4: Alert Fatigue at SafeOps
*   **Challenge**: Engineers ignored critical alerts due to notification spam.
*   **Solution**: Implemented Smart Grouping and Priority Routing.
*   **Result**: 50% reduction in total alerts; 100% response rate to critical safety warnings.
