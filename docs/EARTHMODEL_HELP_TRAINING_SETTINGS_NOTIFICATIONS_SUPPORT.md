# Support Channels & SLAs

## Support Channels
1.  **Knowledge Base**: 24/7 Self-service via Help Center.
2.  **Community Forum**: Peer-to-peer support.
3.  **Chatbot**: Automated instant assistance.
4.  **Email Support**: `support@petrolord.com` for non-urgent issues.
5.  **Live Chat**: For Enterprise customers (Business Hours).
6.  **Phone**: Critical severity incidents only (Enterprise Elite).

## Service Level Agreements (SLAs)

| Severity | Response Time (Standard) | Response Time (Enterprise) | Resolution Target |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Critical** | 4 Hours | 1 Hour | 24 Hours |
| **High** | 8 Hours | 4 Hours | 3 Business Days |
| **Medium** | 24 Hours | 12 Hours | Next Release |
| **Low** | Best Effort | 24 Hours | Roadmap |

## Support Levels
*   **Level 1**: Automated / Self-Service / Community.
*   **Level 2**: Junior Support Agents (General usage, configuration).
*   **Level 3**: Senior Engineers (Bugs, data corruption).
