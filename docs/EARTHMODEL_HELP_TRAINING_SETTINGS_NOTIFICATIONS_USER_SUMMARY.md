# User Summary: What's New in Support & Training

Welcome to the enhanced EarthModel Pro support experience!

**For Everyone**:
*   **Easier Help**: Just press `?` or click the icon to find answers instantly. The new AI chatbot can guide you through complex tasks.
*   **Stay Informed**: The new Notification Center keeps you updated on your projects without spamming your inbox. Configure your preferences in Settings.

**For Learners**:
*   **Training Hub**: Visit the Training Hub to start your journey towards EarthModel Certification. Earn badges and track your skills.

**For Admins**:
*   **Control**: New Enterprise Settings give you command over security, integrations, and team permissions.
