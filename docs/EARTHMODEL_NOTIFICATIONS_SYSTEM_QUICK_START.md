# Quick Start: Notifications

1.  **Check**: Look at the **Bell Icon**. Is there a red dot?
2.  **View**: Click the bell to open the dropdown.
3.  **Filter**: Click "Alerts" to see only system warnings.
4.  **Configure**: Click the "Settings" gear *inside* the notification dropdown.
5.  **Disable**: Uncheck "Marketing Updates" to stop receiving news.
6.  **Done**: Close the panel.
