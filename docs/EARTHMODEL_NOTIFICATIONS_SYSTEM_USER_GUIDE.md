# Notifications System User Guide

## 1. Notification Center
Click the **Bell Icon** to open the Notification Center.
*   **Unread**: Dots indicate unread items.
*   **Actions**: Click a notification to jump to the relevant context (e.g., a specific well).
*   **Filters**: Tabs allow filtering by "All", "Mentions", "System", and "Alerts".

## 2. Managing Alerts
*   **Dismiss**: Swipe or click 'x' to remove notifications.
*   **Mark Read**: "Mark all as read" clears the badge count.
*   **Archive**: Saved important notifications for later reference.

## 3. Custom Rules
Create custom monitoring rules in **Settings > Notifications > Rules**.
*   *Example*: "If [Well A] [Depth] increases by [100ft], send [SMS]."
*   *Example*: "If [Simulation] [Fails], send [Email] to [Manager]."

## 4. Do Not Disturb
Enable DND to pause non-critical notifications.
*   **Schedule**: Set automatic DND hours (e.g., 10 PM - 6 AM).
*   **Exceptions**: Allow "Critical Severity" alerts to bypass DND.

## 5. Troubleshooting
*   **Not receiving emails?** Check your Spam folder and ensure your email address is verified in Profile Settings.
*   **Too many alerts?** Use the "Group by Source" setting to bundle notifications.
