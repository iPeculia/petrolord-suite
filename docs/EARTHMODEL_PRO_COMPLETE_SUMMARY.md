# EarthModel Pro - Complete Product Summary

## Vision
EarthModel Pro is the premier integrated subsurface platform, combining geological modeling, reservoir engineering, and project economics into a single, cloud-native environment. With the completion of Phase 4, it now embeds state-of-the-art Machine Learning to automate complex tasks and reveal deeper insights.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Geology & Geophysics
*   2D/3D Seismic Visualization & Interpretation
*   Well Correlation & Stratigraphy
*   Geological Mapping & Contouring
*   **New in v4**: Automated Facies Classification, Fault Detection

### 2. Reservoir Engineering
*   Volumetrics & Uncertainty Analysis
*   Material Balance & Flow Simulation Interface
*   PVT Analysis
*   **New in v4**: Property Prediction, Auto-Calibration

### 3. Drilling & Completions
*   Well Planning & Trajectory Design
*   Torque & Drag, Hydraulics
*   Casing Design
*   **New in v4**: Well Placement Optimization

### 4. Economics & Planning
*   Field Development Planning (FDP)
*   NPV & Cash Flow Analysis
*   Portfolio Management

## Architecture
Built on a modern React tech stack with a Supabase backend, EarthModel Pro ensures:
*   **Scalability**: Cloud-native design.
*   **Security**: Enterprise-grade RLS and authentication.
*   **Performance**: Optimized for web delivery of heavy scientific data.

## Conclusion
EarthModel Pro v4.0.0 represents a significant leap forward, offering a truly intelligent workspace for energy professionals.
