# Quick Start: Training System

1.  **Navigate**: Go to **Settings > Training Hub**.
2.  **Browse**: Look at the "Recommended for You" section.
3.  **Start**: Click on "EarthModel Pro Essentials".
4.  **Learn**: Watch the first 5-minute video module.
5.  **Quiz**: Answer the 3-question check at the end.
6.  **Progress**: Verify your progress bar has updated to 10%.
