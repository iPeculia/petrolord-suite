# Training Troubleshooting

| Issue | Possible Cause | Solution |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Progress stuck at 99%** | Did not watch video to the very end. | Let video play until the final second. |
| **Quiz won't load** | Session timeout. | Refresh page and log in again. |
| **Certificate missing** | Processing delay. | Wait 15 minutes after course completion. Check email. |
