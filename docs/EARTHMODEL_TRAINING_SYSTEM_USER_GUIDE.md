# Training System User Guide

## 1. Getting Started
Navigate to **Settings > Training** or click the graduation cap icon to enter the Training Hub. This is your personal dashboard for learning.

## 2. Courses and Modules
*   **Browsing**: Filter courses by difficulty (Beginner, Advanced), Role (Geologist, Driller), or Topic.
*   **Enrolling**: Click "Start Course" to begin. Progress is saved automatically.
*   **Structure**: Courses consist of video lectures, reading materials, and interactive exercises.

## 3. Skill Assessments
Take a Skill Assessment to benchmark your knowledge. The system will recommend a tailored Learning Path based on your gaps (e.g., "Recommended: Advanced Geomechanics").

## 4. Certifications
Upon completing a Learning Path, you may be eligible for certification.
1.  Complete all required coursework.
2.  Pass the final proctored exam with >80%.
3.  Download your digital certificate and badge for your profile.

## 5. Interactive Workshops
These are special modules where you perform tasks in a safe "sandbox" version of EarthModel Pro.
*   Follow step-by-step prompts.
*   System verifies your actions (e.g., "Successfully picked a horizon").
*   Receive instant feedback on accuracy.

## 6. Tips
*   **Set Goals**: Use the dashboard to set weekly learning time goals.
*   **Join Groups**: Use the Peer Learning tab to find others studying similar topics.
